travelogue

Enjoy this travelogue, of sorts, of some of my favorite views from around the world. Shot on 35mm film + digital.
Marble mountains in the Sahara Desert, Niger, 2012
Sandstorms can wipe out your vision and slow your day for hours, forcing people inside home or inside cloth veils. Niger, 2012

The dark red sand of Southern Niger holds fertile promise for farmers, who can grow vegetables, grains, and fruits in abundance with the proper irrigation systems. Niger, 2012
Nigerien soliders are just as excited to visit the Timia Oasis in Northern Niger. The town hasn't seen many tourists since al-Qaeda began operating in the Sahara, and they are happy for a visit from the Prime Minister and American delagation. The military has advanced the area before our arrival. Niger, 2012
