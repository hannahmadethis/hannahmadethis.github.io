about me

I'm Hannah.

I'm a creative and innovator who wants to make a difference in the world. You'll rarely find me without multiple projects going at a time, whether it's writing, knitting, drawing, painting, web design, or pixel art. I love language and traveling, and I'm an avid reader and info-seeker.

I'm an INFP and really embody this personality type. I'm quiet, but there is always a firestorm in my mind. I have trouble resting when there are injustices around me. I take on too many responsibilities and hold myself and others to a high standard. However, I understand and empathize with people's struggles, and I feel compelled to help in any way possible.

Professionally, I find my footing in work that allows me to collaborate with people and mutually grow. I want to make a positive impact on the world. I do this through compassionate teamwork and an open mind. I thrive in a fast-paced work environment with new experiences and opportunities. I strive to make improvements to broken systems, and I care about workflow and aesthetics.

I earned my bachelor's degrees in chemistry and anthropology, because I love science but I'm fascinated by people. I completed my master's degree in sociocultural anthropology, with a focus on international development and medical anthropology. I have work experience in the fields of:

  • Writing
  • Editing
  • Qualitative Research
  • Academic Publishing
  • Refugee Resettlement
  • Teaching English to speakers of other languages
  • After-school & international education programs
  • International aid
  • Tutoring
  • Mentorship for undergraduate students
