about me

I'm Hannah.

I'm a creative who wants to make a difference in the world. You'll rarely find me without multiple projects going at a time, whether it's writing, knitting, drawing, painting, web design, or pixel art.

I earned my bachelor's degrees in chemistry and anthropology, and I completed my master's degree in sociocultural anthropology. I have work experience in the fields of:

  • Writing
  • Editing
  • Qualitative Research
  • Academic Publishing
  • Refugee Resettlement
  • Teaching English to speakers of other languages
  • After-school Education programs
  • Tutoring
  • Mentorship for undergraduate students
